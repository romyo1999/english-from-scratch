what is the difference between a & an

read these examples :

  • You are an angry person
  • You are fate like an elephant
  • Your voice like afrog
  • are you a donkey

so why sometimes we use a and sometimes we use an

we use a

  • when the noun is singular :
    • you are a sad people
    • you are a sad person
  • when the noun has a plural and singular
    • you are like a honey
    • i am a girl

we use an

  • when the noun is singular :
    • i saw an owls
    • i saw an owl
  • when the noun has a plural and singular
    • you are beautiful like an oxygen
    • i ate an apple
  • the first letter in the noun :
    • start with a ,e,u,o,i with the same pronunciation
    • or any letter that has the same pronunciation

    How to make Question & How to answer it

    yes / no Question

    replace am with I and add ?
    replace is with she & he & it and add ?
    replace are with you & we & we and add ?

    the sentence ========>> yes / no Question
    you are my friend ========>> are you my friend ?
    I am hungry ========>> am i hungry ?
    he is better ========>> is he better ?
    it is dead ========>> is it dead ?
    he is mychael ========>> is he mychael ?
    they are teachers ========>> are they teachers ?

    yes answer

    yes, + i am
    yes, + ( she & he & it ) + is
    yes, + ( you & we & they )+ are

    Question yes Answer 1 yes Answer 2
    are you my friend ? yes, iam your friend yes, iam
    am i hungry ? yes, you are hungry yes, you are
    is he better ? yes, he is better yes, he is
    is it dead ? yes, it is dead yes, it is
    is he mychael ? yes, he is mychael yes, he is
    are they teachers ? yes, they are teachers yes, they are

    no answer

    no, + i am not
    no, + ( she & he & it ) + is not
    no, + (you & we & they)+ are not

    Question no Answer 1 no Answer 2 no Answer 3
    are you my friend ? no, iam not your friend no, iam not no, iam not
    am i hungry ? no, you are not hungry no, you are not no , you aren't
    is he better ? no, he is not better no, he is not no, he isn't
    is it dead ? no, it is not dead no, it is not no, it isn't
    is he mychael ? no, he is not mychael no, he is not no, he isn't
    are they teachers ? no, they are not teachers no, they are not no, they aren't

    Wh-Questions How& Who& Why& Where& When& What

    how

    How + (are & am & is) + noun + ?

      Examples:
    • how are you ?
    • how is she ?
    • how is mary ?
    • how am i ?
    • how are people there ?

    Who

    Who + (are & am & is) + noun + ?

      Examples:
    • Who are you ?
    • Who is she ?
    • Who is mary ?
    • Who am i ?
    • Who are those people ?

    Why

    Why + (are & (me/am) & is) + noun + ?

      Examples:
    • Why are you here ?
    • Why is she crying ?
    • Why is mary laughing ?
    • Why me ?
    • Why am i the only genius ?
    • Why are tholyse people ?

    Where

    Why + (are & am & is) + noun + ?

      Examples:
    • Where are you ?
    • Where is she ?
    • Where is mary ?
    • Where am i ?
    • Where are they ?

    What

    Why + (are & am & is) + noun + ?

      Examples:
    • what are you doing ?
    • what is she playing ?
    • what is mary making ?
    • what am i doing?
    • what are they carrying ?
