we use a & an & the

  • adjective + noun
    • she is a beautiful girl
    • it is a big university
    • i made a new friend
    • like an angry bullet
    • you are an evil man
    • she is an ugly girl
  • when the noun is singular
    • she is an ugly girl
    • they are ugly girls
    • i made a new friend
    • i made new friends
    • like an angry bullet
    • like angry bullets
    • you are an evil man
    • you are evil men

    nouns type

    countable noun

  • countable noun has singular so we use a / an
    • three girls
    • a beautiful girl
    • four universitys
    • a big university
    • five friends
    • a new friend
    • 20 bullets
    • an angry bullet
    • siven men
    • an evil man

    • uncountable noun

  • uncountable noun has no singular so we don't use a / an
    • i can't eat butter
    • is that goat cheese ?
    • i need food
    • all i have is cold milk
    • give me sugar

    • proper noun

  • proper noun has no singular so we don't use a / an and the first leter uppercase
    • you come with us to Morocco
    • i like Facebook ?
    • i hate Hitler
    • do you love Coca kola

    • volatile noun

  • sometimes countable and sometimes uncountable
    • we have three cows and twelve chickens in our farm
    • it's achicken
    • do you want to eat chicken ?
    • i made chicken ?
    • i hateHitler
    • do you love coca kola
